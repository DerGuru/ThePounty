## Haupt-Blurb (Amazon-Produktseite / Backcover)

**Number 4 Maple Lane has a new neighbor, and everyone agrees he is the most courteous man on the street.**

Aldric Venn mows his lawn at nine o'clock sharp. He learns every name and uses it, every time. He returns the package you didn't know was missing, brings the right wine to dinner without being told what's cooking, and apologizes for things that were never his fault.

Dave thinks he's a little odd. Rosa thinks he's lonely. Sandra thinks he'd be perfect for the HOA. Only Gerald Hooper, the retired auditor three doors down, thinks something doesn't add up.

And Gerald keeps notebooks.

Because Aldric Venn has a great many rules. About lawns. About names. About the precise, unfailing courtesy he extends to every soul on Maple Lane. He follows all of them. He never explains the one beneath them all:

*Good men don't need them.*

For one year, the street will host its barbecues and birthdays, its small feuds and quiet kindnesses, never quite seeing the man at its center. Because courtesy, on Maple Lane, is a discipline. And discipline, kept long enough, becomes a kind of warning.

Some debts get settled on this street. The neighbors just never see the bill.

## Notizen

- **Voice:** trocken, präzise, dramatische Ironie — spiegelt den Buchton.
- **Genre-Tags (offensiv vermarkten):** slow burn · character-driven · cozy menace · suburban suspense. Ehrliches Erwartungs-Management = Zielgruppen-Filter (zieht geduldige Slow-Burn-/Read-Through-Leser an, hält Action-Sucher fern → weniger Fehlkäufe, bessere Reviews).

- **Reviews/Quotes:** sobald vorhanden, oberhalb des Blurbs platzieren (1–2 kurze Zeilen).
- **A/B-Test:** Erste Zeile + Kurz-Hook für Amazon-Ads gegeneinander testen.
